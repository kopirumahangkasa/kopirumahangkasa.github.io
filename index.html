About Us

Serving Since 2023

Our Story

Located in the heart of a peaceful residential neighborhood, our café offers a homey, calm, and cozy atmosphere, perfect for unwinding. The name Rumah Angkasa is inspired by the owner's child's name, reflecting the warmth and personal touch we bring to every cup of coffee. At Rumah Angkasa, we are not just about selling coffee; we are passionate about educating everyone that anyone can make a great cup of coffee. Our café is a place where we share the love and knowledge of coffee, empowering people to explore and appreciate the art of brewing.

Our Vision

Our café is committed to growing beyond serving just coffee. We aim to produce our own coffee beans, ensuring the highest quality, and expand our reach by supplying our beans to locations far and wide. We envision Rumah Angkasa becoming a key player in the coffee industry, known not only for our exceptional beverages but also for our contributions to the coffee supply chain.

Our Services

Fresh & Organic Beans

Fastest Door Delivery

With fast delivery service, Kopi Rumah Angkasa ensures that your coffee reaches you quickly without compromising freshness. The door delivery service allows customers to enjoy their coffee without leaving home, making the coffee-buying experience more practical and efficient.

Fresh Coffee Beans

Kopi Rumah Angkasa uses carefully selected fresh and organic coffee beans. These beans not only offer richer flavor but are also free from harmful chemicals commonly found in non-organic coffee. By using fresh and organic beans, each cup of coffee served delivers a more authentic and natural taste, providing a truly satisfying coffee experience.

Best Quality Coffee

Committed to the highest quality, Kopi Rumah Angkasa only serves premium coffee that meets the highest standards. This ensures every customer gets the best tasting coffee with every cup. Kopi Rumah Angkasa offers a wide selection of coffee flavors, catering to various preferences, providing many options for coffee lovers.

Online Table Booking

Kopi Rumah Angkasa makes hosting events or gatherings easy with its online table booking system. Whether you're planning a small meeting, a family gathering, or a larger celebration, the system allows you to reserve space at the café in advance.
